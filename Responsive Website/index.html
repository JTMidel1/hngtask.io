My Two Years Goal in Tech

My goal is to become a web and AI enthusiast within the next two years. I am currently immersing myself in web technologies to grasp the inner workings of the web. My aspiration is to further delve into other cutting-edge technologies and their seamless integration with AI, ultimately achieving a profound and comprehensive understanding in this exciting field. To accomplish this, I plan to build a solid foundation by mastering web development principles, frameworks, and best practices, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and various libraries and frameworks like React and Angular. I will also explore backend technologies such as Node.js, databases, and server management to gain a full-stack perspective. Subsequently, I will dive into AI concepts, focusing on machine learning, neural networks, data analysis, and natural language processing. I intend to learn and utilize popular AI frameworks and tools such as TensorFlow, PyTorch, and scikit-learn. By combining these skill sets, I aim to create innovative solutions that leverage the power of AI within web applications, contributing meaningfully to the ever-evolving tech landscape. I am committed to continuous learning and staying updated with the latest advancements, attending workshops, participating in online courses, and collaborating with like-minded professionals. This journey will enable me to not only understand but also influence the future of technology, bridging the gap between web development and artificial intelligence.
